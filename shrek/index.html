About this character

This is "Shrek". He is an ogre. He's all big and green and no body likes him much, so he lives away from the kingdom, deep in the forest.

Despite his wierd appearance, he is the hero who falls in love with the lovely "Princess Fiona".

Along with "Donkey", the most hillarious being in the kingdom, they set on a journey of adventures that goes through amazing incidents all along.
